History of Book Galore

Book Galore began its journey in the heart of Delhi in the year 2012. Founded by Pradeep, a lifelong book enthusiast, the store quickly gained a reputation for its diverse collection and welcoming atmosphere. Initially a brick-and-mortar establishment, Book Galore expanded its reach in 2017 by launching its online platform, making it accessible to readers across the country. With a commitment to providing a curated selection of books and exceptional customer service, Book Galore has grown into a beloved destination for book lovers of all ages.
